FitRing

FitRing is a design proposal for a ring-shaped wearable made for runners. It tracks important running data and communicates through vibrations and audio, making it easy to stay focused while running.

It’s designed for runners who run regularly and like listening to music or podcasts while tracking their performance.

Music App

My team and I worked on a post-punk music app. The wireframes were designed in Figma.

Post-punk is a genre known for its melancholic atmosphere, which often combines dark and emotional tones. We aimed to create an app to reflect this mood.

The app is made for anyone who enjoys post-punk music and wants a space dedicated to discovering and listening to it.

Co-Lab Board

Co-Lab Board Game is a project created for Malmö City Library, Sweden. It was designed as a tool that librarians can use to develop, manage, and make decisions about the library’s open and shared spaces.

The game includes a playing board and persona-based game pieces. It supports user-centred, participatory design approach.

Interactivity

In this project, I explored different types of interactivity, including keyboard inputs, Arduino-based inputs, and hand gestures.

The goal was to see how these different input methods change the overall experience and what new possibilities they open up. The focus was also on how closely a digital experience can start to feel like experience from real life. The app is made for anyone who enjoys post-punk music and wants a space dedicated to discovering and listening to it.
